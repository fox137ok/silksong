About Silksong

Hollow Knight: Silksong is the action-adventure sequel developed by Team Cherry. This time you'll play as Princess Hornet on a brand new adventure through the mysterious kingdom of Pharloom.

The game features a new combat system, silk manipulation mechanics, and a world larger and more complex than its predecessor. Christopher Larkin once again composed mesmerizing original music.
