Team Cherry Major Development Progress Update

2024-12-15 · Official

Team Cherry has shared a long‑awaited development report confirming that Hollow Knight: Silksong has entered its final phase. Core content is complete, and the team is now focusing on optimization, testing, and polish.

What’s In The Final Phase

  • Performance sweeps across major regions in Pharloom.
  • Passes on enemy balance, boss scripting, and damage windows.
  • Localization, platform certification, and accessibility checks.

Platforms And Launch

The game is slated to launch on PC, Nintendo Switch, PlayStation, and Xbox. The team reiterates quality targets over date pressure.

The world of Pharloom is larger and more intricate than initially planned—optimizations ensure the experience remains fluid and responsive.
