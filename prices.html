Steam Purchase FAQ

Q: Why do prices vary so much between regions?

A: Steam uses regional pricing strategies, setting different prices based on each region's economic level and purchasing power, resulting in significant price differences.

Q: Will using VPN for purchases result in account bans?

A: Steam has some tolerance for VPN purchases, but frequent or malicious region changes may risk account bans. Use responsibly and follow relevant guidelines.

Q: Do games from low-price regions have regional locks?

A: Most Steam games don't have strict regional locks, but some games may have runtime restrictions. Check the game page's regional support information before purchasing.

Q: How to choose the most suitable purchase region?

A: Consider factors like price, payment convenience, regional locks, and risks comprehensively. For beginners, we recommend choosing relatively safe moderate-price regions.
